1. PURPOSE

This Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Hey Ralli, LLC, a Tennessee limited liability company ("Company"), and the individual or entity identified on the Signature Page ("Receiving Party").

Hey Ralli develops proprietary software, artificial intelligence technologies, business systems, creative assets, and operational methodologies. During the course of evaluating, testing, advising, supporting, or performing services for the Company, the Receiving Party may obtain access to confidential and proprietary information.

The purpose of this Agreement is to protect those assets and establish the Receiving Party's obligations regarding their use and disclosure.

2. DEFINITIONS

For purposes of this Agreement:

Company means Hey Ralli, LLC.

Receiving Party means the individual or entity executing this Agreement.

Confidential Information has the meaning set forth in Section 3.

4. RECEIVING PARTY OBLIGATIONS

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  • Maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information.
  • Use Confidential Information solely for authorized work benefiting Hey Ralli.
  • Prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Promptly report any suspected security incident or unauthorized disclosure.
  • Exercise reasonable care consistent with industry standards to protect Company information.

5. PLATFORM & SYSTEM ACCESS

Any access granted to Company systems—including repositories, cloud services, APIs, databases, staging environments, documentation, analytics platforms, and administrative tools—is provided solely for authorized business purposes.

The Receiving Party shall not:

  • Share login credentials.
  • Circumvent security controls.
  • Copy proprietary information for personal use.
  • Use Company systems outside the scope of authorized work.

The Company reserves the right to revoke access at any time.

6. OWNERSHIP

All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of Hey Ralli.

Nothing contained in this Agreement grants any ownership, license, patent rights, copyright interests, trademark rights, or other intellectual property rights.

Ownership of work created for the Company is governed by the separate Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement.

7. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon request or upon termination of the Parties' relationship, the Receiving Party shall promptly:

  • Return all Company property.
  • Permanently delete Confidential Information stored electronically.
  • Destroy printed confidential materials.
  • Discontinue access to Company systems.

8. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

  • Is publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
  • Was lawfully known before disclosure;
  • Is independently developed without use of Company Confidential Information; or
  • Must be disclosed pursuant to applicable law.

9. TERM

This Agreement becomes effective on the Effective Date and remains in effect for five (5) years after the Parties' relationship ends.

Trade secret obligations survive for as long as permitted under applicable law.

10. REMEDIES

The Receiving Party acknowledges that unauthorized disclosure may cause irreparable harm to Hey Ralli.

The Company may seek injunctive relief, equitable relief, damages, and any other remedies available under applicable law.

11. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Tennessee.

12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

If any provision of this Agreement is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver of future enforcement.

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties concerning confidentiality.
